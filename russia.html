Study MBBS in Russia

How to Apply

  • Choose a university and gather required documents.
  • Submit application form along with academic transcripts.
  • Receive admission letter and apply for a student visa.
  • Complete medical check-up and travel to Russia.

Why Choose Russia?

Affordable Tuition Fees

Russian universities offer cost-effective medical education compared to other countries.

Globally Recognized Degrees

Russian MBBS degrees are recognized by WHO, MCI, and many other medical councils.
